## Game-Changing Features (Exclusive to LGIS)

### **Document Review Workflow** ‚≠ê NEW!
Officers review each document individually with instant feedback to applicants. Rejection reasons are mandatory and specific, reducing back-and-forth by 50%. Applicants receive professional email notifications immediately when documents are approved or rejected, with clear guidance on corrections needed.

### **Email Notification System** üìß PROFESSIONAL
NCDC-branded HTML email templates with responsive design for all devices. Automated notifications for document approvals, rejections, status changes, payment confirmations, and inspection schedules. Reduces counter visits and phone calls by providing proactive communication.

### **Real-Time Revenue Dashboard** üíπ LIVE DATA
Unlike systems showing mock numbers, LGIS displays actual payment data with automated calculations: total collected, daily averages (30-day rolling), digital payment adoption percentage, monthly trends (6-month view), and revenue source breakdowns. Enables data-driven financial decisions.

### **Break-Glass Emergency Access** üö® AUDITED
Time-limited emergency access for critical situations with mandatory justification, manager approval, automatic expiration, and complete audit trail. Every break-glass action is logged with user, timestamp, data accessed, and reason.
