### Reviewing Documents (2 min)
**[Screen: Document review modal - show approve and reject]**

**Narrator**:
"Click the Documents tab, then 'Submitted'. Here are all uploaded documents.

To review, click the arrow icon next to a document. The review modal opens.

If it's an image or PDF, you'll see a preview. Check carefully:
- Is it clear and readable?
- Is it the correct document?
- Is it valid or expired?
- Does it match the applicant's information?

If there's a problem, click 'Reject'. Here's the key part - provide a specific, helpful reason. Don't just say 'unclear' - explain what's wrong and how to fix it.

Click 'Confirm Rejection'. The applicant receives your feedback and can re-upload a corrected version."

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### For Citizens/Business Owners

#### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within 5 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**  
A: Yes! Go to your profile, update your email, and verify the new address via the link sent to it.

**Q: Why can't I login?**  
A: Common reasons: 1) Wrong password (case-sensitive), 2) Email not verified, 3) Account disabled. Try password reset first. Still stuck? Contact support.

#### Applications

**Q: How long does processing take?**  
A: Typical timeline is 5-10 business days, depending on license type and application completeness. Express processing available for urgent cases (extra fee).

**Q: Can I save my application and finish it later?**  
A: Yes! Your progress is automatically saved. Just login and continue where you left off.

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**  
A: No, but you can contact the reviewing officer through the Messages tab if you need to update information.

**Q: What if I uploaded the wrong document?**  
A: If an officer hasn't reviewed it yet, you can delete and re-upload. If already reviewed, wait for feedback and re-upload when prompted.

**Q: How do I know if my application is approved?**  
A: You'll receive an email notification and see status change to "Approved" in your account. You'll then be prompted to pay fees.

#### Documents

**Q: What file types can I upload?**  
A: PDF, JPG, and PNG files only. Maximum 10MB per file.

**Q: My file is too large. What should I do?**  
A: For images: Compress using free online tools. For PDFs: Split into smaller parts or compress. Aim for under 5MB for best results.

**Q: Do I need original documents?**  
A: You upload scanned copies or clear photos. Bring originals to inspection (if required) or license collection.

**Q: Can I upload documents from my phone?**  
A: Yes! LGIS works on mobile browsers. You can take photos and upload directly.

**Q: Why was my document rejected?**  
A: The officer's feedback will explain specifically what was wrong. Common reasons: blurry image, wrong document, expired certificate, missing pages.

#### Payments

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: After your application is approved. You'll receive payment details via email and in your account.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Bank transfer (recommended), cash at NCDC office, mobile money (coming soon).

**Q: Can I get a receipt?**  
A: Yes! After payment verification, download your official receipt from your account.

**Q: What if my application is rejected? Do I get a refund?**  
A: Processing fees are non-refundable, but you can reapply at no additional cost if you fix the issues.

### For Officers

#### System Use

**Q: I can't access an application. Why?**  
A: Possible reasons: 1) Application assigned to different officer, 2) Wrong filter settings, 3) Application archived. Check with supervisor if you should have access.

**Q: Can I reassign an application to another officer?**  
A: Only managers can reassign. If you need to transfer, contact your supervisor.

**Q: How do I handle priority/urgent applications?**  
A: Look for "Express" or "Urgent" flags. Process these first. Contact supervisor if unsure which should be prioritized.

#### Document Review

**Q: What if I accidentally approved a wrong document?**  
A: Contact your supervisor immediately. Only managers can reverse approvals. Document the error.

**Q: Can applicants re-upload after I approve?**  
A: No. Once approved, document is locked. If there's an issue, contact your supervisor.

**Q: What level of detail should I give in rejection reasons?**  
A: Be specific enough that applicant knows exactly what to fix. Good: "Certificate expired Jan 2025 - need current 2026". Bad: "Not good enough".

**Q: What if an applicant keeps uploading wrong documents?**  
A: After 3 rejections, escalate to supervisor. May need phone call to clarify requirements.

#### Workflow

**Q: When should I schedule an inspection?**  
A: When all documents are approved and application details verified. Some license types (like trading) may not need inspection.

**Q: What if an applicant doesn't respond to my requests?**  
A: Send reminder after 3 days. After 7 days of no response, move to "Abandoned" status (supervisor approval needed).

**Q: Can I deny an application?**  
A: Yes, if business doesn't qualify (outside jurisdiction, doesn't meet requirements, etc.). Provide clear reason. Applicant can appeal to your supervisor.

### Technical Issues

**Q: The system is really slow. What's wrong?**  
A: Try: 1) Close unused tabs, 2) Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Del), 3) Use Chrome or Firefox, 4) Check internet speed. Still slow? Report to IT.

**Q: I get an error when trying to upload. Help!**  
A: Check: 1) File size under 10MB, 2) File type is PDF/JPG/PNG, 3) Filename has no special characters. Try different browser if issue persists.

**Q: Changes I made aren't saving.**  
A: Check your internet connection. Make sure you click "Save" button. Try hard refresh (Ctrl+F5). Report persistent issues to IT.

**Q: Can I use LGIS on my tablet/phone?**  
A: Yes! LGIS is mobile-friendly. All features work on tablets and phones through your browser.

### Scenario 1: First-Time Applicant (Citizen Role Play)

**Setup**: New business owner wants trading license

**Student Task**: Guide them through:
1. Account creation
2. Choosing correct license type
3. Completing application
4. Uploading documents
5. Tracking status

**Practice Points**:
- Explain each step clearly
- Anticipate common questions
- Show patience
- Verify understanding

### Scenario 2: Document Rejection (Officer Role Play)

**Setup**: Trading license application, tax certificate is expired

**Student Task**: 
1. Review document
2. Identify issue (expiry date)
3. Write clear rejection reason
4. Submit rejection
5. Handle applicant follow-up question

**Practice Points**:
- Be professional but friendly
- Explain clearly what's needed
- Provide timeline
- Offer to help if confused

### Scenario 3: Handling Difficult Applicant (Officer Role Play)

**Setup**: Applicant insists their blurry document is fine

**Student Task**:
1. Explain quality standards
2. Show examples if possible
3. Remain professional
4. Escalate if needed

**Practice Points**:
- Stay calm
- Don't argue
- Stick to policy
- Know when to escalate
