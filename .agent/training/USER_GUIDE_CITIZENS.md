5. Save changes

## ğŸ” What Happens During Inspection?

### Before the Inspection

You'll receive notification with:
- ğŸ“… Date and time
- ğŸ‘¤ Inspector name
- âœ… Checklist of what will be checked

**Prepare**:
- Make sure premises are clean
- Have all equipment ready
- Be present (or have authorized person)
- Have all documents ready

### During Inspection

Inspector will check:
- **Physical Premises**: Condition, size, location
- **Safety**: Fire extinguishers, emergency exits
- **Equipment**: Kitchen equipment, storage facilities
- **Compliance**: Zoning regulations, building codes
- **Documents**: Originals of submitted documents

**Be Cooperative**:
- Answer questions honestly
- Show inspector around
- Provide requested documents
- Take note of any issues mentioned

### After Inspection

Inspector will:
- Submit report within 2 days
- List any issues found
- Recommend approval or list required fixes

**If Issues Found**:
- You'll get list of things to fix
- Fix them within given timeframe
- Request re-inspection when ready

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### About Applications

**Q: How long does processing take?**  
A: Typically 5-10 business days, depending on license type and completeness of application.

**Q: Can I edit my application after submitting?**  
A: No, but you can contact the reviewing officer through the "Messages" tab.

**Q: What if I made a mistake?**  
A: Contact NCDC support immediately with your application number.

**Q: Can I withdraw my application?**  
A: Yes, go to application details and click "Withdraw". Fees are non-refundable.

### About Documents

**Q: What file types are accepted?**  
A: PDF, JPG, PNG. Maximum 10MB per file.

**Q: Can I upload multiple pages as one document?**  
A: Yes! Combine into single PDF or upload multiple images.

**Q: How long do I have to re-upload rejected documents?**  
A: 7 days. After that, application may be auto-rejected.

**Q: Can I upload documents later?**  
A: Yes, but application won't be processed until all required documents are uploaded.

### About Payments

**Q: Do I pay before or after approval?**  
A: After approval, before license issuance.

**Q: Can I get a refund if rejected?**  
A: Processing fees are non-refundable, but you can reapply.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Bank transfer, cash at NCDC office, mobile money (coming soon).

### Technical Issues

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page, enter your email, and follow reset link.

**Q: My document won't upload. Why?**  
A: Check file size (under 10MB), file type (PDF/JPG/PNG), and internet connection.

**Q: I'm not receiving emails. Help!**  
A: Check spam folder, verify email address in profile, add noreply@ncdc.gov.pg to contacts.

**Q: The website is slow/not loading**  
A: Try clearing browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete), use Chrome or Firefox, check internet connection.
